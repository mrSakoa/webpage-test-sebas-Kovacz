Can Jogging Heal a Broken Soul?

Here at Hells Pass Hospital, were no strangers to the strange. But even we were surprised to see how incorporating light

exercise—like jogging, tai chi, and even dodgeball—has improved patient morale and recovery times.“Sport isnt just for athletes,”

says Dr. Rex Knuckle, our Sports Medicine specialist. “Its medicine in motion.”Recent studies (and one very enthusiastic nurse) show

that patients who engage in supervised movement report lower pain levels and higher optimism.So whether its a brisk walk

or a gentle stretch, your road to healing might just start with a good pair of sneakers. Just dont race the interns. Again.
