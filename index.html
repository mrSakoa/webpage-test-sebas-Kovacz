Laboratory Accreditation

We are proud to announce that our Laboratory has maintained accreditation under the IRAM-ISO 15189

International Standard for the fifth consecutive time since 2019, making us one of only 12 accredited

labs in the country. This ensures continued quality in our results, reinforces our commitment to patients,

and reflects our ongoing improvement aligned with top international standards. Congratulations to the

entire team on this achievement!

New MRI Machine Installed

Hells pass Hospital has installed a cutting-edge MRI machine to improve diagnostic speed and accuracy

Dr. Lisa Carter, Chief Radiologist, says the new system will reduce wait times and provide clearer imaging.

Made possible by community donations and a health grant, the machine begins operation Monday.
